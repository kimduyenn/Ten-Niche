Taurine

ENERGY
TAURINE
Amino sulfonic acid (conditionally essential)
Amino Acid Complex
One of the most abundant amino acids in the body — found naturally in muscle, brain, and heart tissue. Works synergistically with caffeine to smooth and extend its energizing effects.
⚙ Mechanism
Regulates calcium signaling in cells, enhancing mitochondrial energy output. Acts as an osmoregulator, protecting cells from oxidative stress. Modulates GABA receptors to calm overstimulation from caffeine.

ENERGY
D-GLUCURONOLACTONE
Natural metabolite of glucose
Detox Compound
A naturally occurring compound derived from glucose metabolism in the liver. A classic ingredient found in premium energy formulations for its detoxifying and stamina-supporting properties.
⚙ Mechanism
Supports glucuronidation — a key liver detox pathway — helping the body clear metabolic waste. May reduce fatigue byproducts in muscle tissue and support glycogen utilization for sustained output.

Detox Support Stamina Mental Clarity Synergy Boost

Green Tea

ENERGY
GREEN TEA EXTRACT
From: Camellia sinensis (Camellia Sinensis)
Extract Standardized
Concentrated extract of green tea, rich in EGCG catechins — one of nature's most studied antioxidants. Provides clean, plant-based energy alongside caffeine for a powerful synergistic focus stack.
⚙ Mechanism
EGCG inhibits the COMT enzyme, prolonging norepinephrine activity in the brain and fat cells. This mildly increases thermogenesis and mental energy, amplifying the effect of caffeine without adding more stimulant.

VITAMINS
VITAMIN B3
Nicotinamide (Niacin)
Form: Nicotinamide
Also known as Niacin — a cornerstone of energy metabolism. In its nicotinamide form, it delivers all the cellular benefits without the flush sensation of regular niacin.
⚙ Mechanism
Essential for NAD+ and NADH synthesis — the electron carriers powering every cell's energy production (ATP). Also supports DNA repair and regulates stress response pathways.
✦ Benefits
Energy Metabolism NAD+ Boost Brain Function

Vitamin B5

VITAMINS
VITAMIN B5
D-Calcium Pantothenate
Form: Calcium Pantothenate
Pantothenic acid — the "anti-stress vitamin." Essential for adrenal gland function and the production of coenzyme A, which is central to fatty acid metabolism and energy release.
⚙ Mechanism
Precursor to Coenzyme A (CoA) — required for converting carbohydrates, fats, and proteins into usable energy (acetyl-CoA). Supports adrenal hormone synthesis to regulate the body's stress response.
✦ Benefits
Adrenal Support Fat Metabolism Stress Resilience

Vitamin B6
Upload vitb6.jpg
VITAMINS
VITAMIN B6
Pyridoxine Hydrochloride
Form: Pyridoxine HCl
The neurotransmitter vitamin. B6 is involved in over 100 enzymatic reactions in the body and is critical for synthesizing serotonin, dopamine, and GABA — your feel-good brain chemicals.
⚙ Mechanism
Acts as a coenzyme in the synthesis of serotonin (from tryptophan), dopamine (from L-DOPA), and GABA. Regulates homocysteine, protecting brain blood vessels. Essential for red blood cell production carrying oxygen to the brain.
✦ Benefits
Mood Regulation Serotonin Brain Health
